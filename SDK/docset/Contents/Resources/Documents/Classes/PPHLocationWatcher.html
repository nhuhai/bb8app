updatePeriodically:withMaximumInterval:

Trigger periodic updates. The watcher uses an adaptive strategy based on whether results are changing or not. So for example, if it calls twice in the minimumUpdateInterval and the tabs have not changed, it will start towards maximumUpdateIntervalSeconds. When results change, it will come back towards minimumUpdateIntervalSeconds.

- (void)updatePeriodically:(NSInteger)minimumUpdateIntervalSeconds withMaximumInterval:(NSInteger)maximumUpdateIntervalSeconds

Parameters

minimumUpdateIntervalSeconds

After a successful call, wait at least this long until the next call

maximumUpdateIntervalSeconds

After a successful call, wait no longer than this long until the next call

Discussion

Trigger periodic updates. The watcher uses an adaptive strategy based on whether results are changing or not. So for example, if it calls twice in the minimumUpdateInterval and the tabs have not changed, it will start towards maximumUpdateIntervalSeconds. When results change, it will come back towards minimumUpdateIntervalSeconds.

A good strategy is to call this method on your “order entry” page or main page with a reasonably high maximum interval and then when you expect to be processing a payment, to call it again with lower values. Last caller wins. Also, update will be called immediately upon this call.
